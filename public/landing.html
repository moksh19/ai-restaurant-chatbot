How it works

  1. Create a restaurant in the dashboard.
  2. Import your menu from an image, PDF, or website URL.
  3. Review and tweak items in the Menu Editor.
  4. Copy the embed code from your restaurant card.
  5. Paste it into your website’s HTML (or send it to your web developer).
